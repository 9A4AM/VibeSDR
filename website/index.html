How it's made

Vibe‑Coded, but not slop.

I'm not a licensed amateur and I don't transmit — I'm a listener, fascinated by radio and the dials that got me interested in the first place. VibeSDR is the receiver I'd have loved to own.

Every feature was designed and personally tested on real hardware across five devices — from an iPhone 17 Pro Max down to a Moto G35 and an iPhone SE. If the GPU waterfall and the NEON DSP hold up on those, they hold up anywhere. I don't ship things I wouldn't use myself.

The code was written with AI assistance (Claude) — hence the name. I designed it, I broke it, I tested it, I filed the bugs and I signed off every release. The full source is public under GPL-3.0, dead ends included — because the honest thing is to say it on the tin and then let people check.
